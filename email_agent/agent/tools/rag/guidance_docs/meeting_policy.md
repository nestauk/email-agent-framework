# Meeting Policy

## Default Meeting Location
All internal team meetings are held at **Nesta HQ, 58 Victoria Embankment, London EC4Y 0DS** unless otherwise specified.

## Meeting Rooms
- Room A (Ground Floor) - capacity 6 people
- Room B (First Floor) - capacity 12 people
- The Boardroom (Second Floor) - capacity 20 people, requires booking 48h in advance

## Virtual Meetings
For remote attendees, all meetings include a Google Meet link sent with the calendar invite.

## Booking Policy
- Book meeting rooms via the shared calendar
- Cancel at least 2 hours in advance if not needed
- External visitors must be registered at reception

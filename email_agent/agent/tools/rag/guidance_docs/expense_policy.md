# Expense Policy

## Approval Thresholds

- Under £100: No approval needed, submit receipt via expense system
- £100-£500: Line manager approval required
- Over £500: Director approval required

## Travel

- Book trains via the corporate travel portal for automatic approval
- Flights require pre-approval regardless of cost
- Standard class only unless journey exceeds 4 hours

## Meals & Entertainment

- Client meals: Up to £50 per person
- Team lunches: Up to £25 per person
- All receipts must be itemised

## Submission Deadline

All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred.
